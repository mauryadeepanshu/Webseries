The story of a small town and its terrifying supernatural events.

Eleven's Story

Eleven, a young girl with psychokinetic abilities, escapes from a secretive government lab and is taken in by a group of kids in Hawkins, Indiana. She helps them search for their missing friend, and together they uncover a parallel dimension called the Upside Down. Eleven's struggle to control her powers and understand her past unfolds alongside the battle to stop the sinister forces from taking over their town.

Mike Wheeler's Story

Mike Wheeler, one of the main group of kids in Hawkins, is a loyal friend who becomes one of Eleven's closest allies. Mike is determined to help Eleven understand her powers, while also dealing with the disappearance of his friend, Will. As the series progresses, Mike grows into a brave young leader, navigating the dangerous threats from the Upside Down and the challenges of growing up.
