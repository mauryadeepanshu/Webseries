Friends

The story of six friends living in New York City.

Monica and Ross

Monica Geller, a chef with a competitive spirit, and her brother Ross, a paleontologist with a knack for awkwardness, navigate life and relationships in New York City. Monica’s journey revolves around her desire for perfection, both in her career and personal life, while Ross faces his own struggles, from divorces to his on-again, off-again romance with Rachel. Their sibling bond brings humor and heart to the group dynamic.

IMDb Rating: 8.9/10

Rachel, Joey, Chandler, and Phoebe

Rachel Green starts as a spoiled runaway bride who finds independence and friendship. Joey Tribbiani, an aspiring actor, brings humor and charm, while Chandler Bing’s sarcasm and wit make him the group’s comic relief. Phoebe Buffay, the quirky and free-spirited musician, adds a unique perspective. Together, their lives intertwine in hilarious and heartfelt ways, making their Central Perk hangouts iconic.

IMDb Rating: 8.9/10
